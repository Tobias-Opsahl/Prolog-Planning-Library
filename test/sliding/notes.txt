The sliding folder implements the sliding puzzle as a domain, corresponding
to the 8 puzzle or the 15 puzzle, or any other size. The h_manhattan heuristics
is specifically designed for this domain, see the file for more documentation. 
It works much faster than the other heuristics and is admissible.
